Week 1 Notes

  • I think it is interesting how in just a few years (since 2011), the paradigm shift of having a computer went from one computer desktop per household to being 2-3 devices per person in the household. The devices being a smart phone usually one per person, maybe a tablet f or the parents and usually another device of some sort.
  • Be There. Be Useful. Be Quick.
  • Multiple steps can frustrate you. 0 steps make you happy without even thinking about it.
  • Speed Matters. People noticed the half second delay it takes for things to load.
  • Fingers Matter. We want to design with the most convenient placement of fingers.
  • Size Matters. Reduce un-needed white space to reduce size.
  • There are better ways to do things.
  • Advertise not Annoy. NO POP-UPS
